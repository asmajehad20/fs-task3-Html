Welcome to Tea+, A Bubble Tea Paradise!

At Tea+, we believe that a perfect cup of tea can turn any moment into something special. Our bubble tea is made with love and care, using premium teas and fresh ingredients to bring you a burst of flavor in every sip Whether you’re a fan of classic milk teas or like to mix it up with fruity blends, there’s something for everyone here. Each cup is customizable, so you can choose your favorite tea base, sweetness level, and of course, those irresistibly chewy boba pearls.

Our cozy tea house is more than just a place to grab a drink – it’s a space to relax, catch up with friends, or even enjoy a quiet moment to yourself. We’ve created an inviting atmosphere where the hustle of daily life melts away as soon as you step inside. Whether you’re here for a quick pick-me-up or looking to stay awhile, we’re ready to serve up happiness, one bubble tea at a time.
